Retro Arcade Machine

In April 2020, my friends and I built a 3x4.5 ft wooden retro arcade machine inspired by the vintage arcades of the 60's. We wanted to recreate the nostalgia associated with the still popular pixel art design that lies at the heart of many past and present games. The machine features the traditional single and multiplayer games Snake, Pong, Pac-Man, and a custom street fighter inspired game.

Click here to see our arcade machine and recorded demos of the games, and here for our GitHub repo. Enjoy!
